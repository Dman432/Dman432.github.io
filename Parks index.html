About MTG

MTG or Magic the Gathering is a card game that involves mythical creatures and as predicted Magic. Mtg was first created by Richard garfield in 1993, and published by Wizards of the Coast and is Still played today. MTG has five main colors that pretty much define it, red, white ,blue, black, and green. These are the five colors and they can be mixed into combinations or multicolored decks. MTG IS a game that I enjoy playing a lot because there are multiple ways to win the game. There's milling which is when you make the person discard their deck into the graveyard, Or there is a way to win by life were you gain or have more life than everyone, etc.
